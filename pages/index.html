Montepulciano Wine grape

Montepulciano wines are famous for their soft flavours and deep colour pigments. Delicate tannins and a low acidity create a wine typically best consumed soon after bottling. Read more

Sangiovese Wine grape

Sangiovese wine is known for its high acidity, medium-plus tannins and savoury characters. Sniff a glass next time you'll find that aromas move toward dried cherries, figs and roses. Read more

shiraz Wine grape

Syrah or shiraz can produce delicously dark wine. Its high tannins means shiraz wines are capable of aging and improving over the decades. Expect rich, deep flavour of dark fruits and hint of coffee and spice. Read more

merlot Wine grape

Merlot's soft texture helps to give it a deliciously plummy, almost fruitcake-like flavour. It can be a little grassy and develops blackcurrant, blackberry, blueberry, chocolate and spice-like characters when fully ripe. Read more

trebbiano Wine grape

Trebbiano d'Abruzzo is a white wine full of colour, sweet scents, duration of flavour and capability to age. In the right hands, the trebbiano grapes results in some of the finest white wines avaliavlible. Read more

moscato Wine grape

The wine made from Moscato has an irresistible sweetness and refreshing fruit flavors! Most Moscato wines have high residual sugar levels, an opulent floral aroma with hints of honeysuckle, and flavor notes of white peach, apricot, and tropical fruit. Read more
